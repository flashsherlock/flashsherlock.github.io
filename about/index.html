About

Me

I’m a graduate student, studying human olfaction with these nice people in Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my BS in Psychology from Beijing Normal University. My favorite psychologist is B.F. Skinner. I was intrigued by his operant conditioning experiments when I read a textbook about animal behavior in high school. Currently, I am using fMRI and iEEG techniques to investigate neural representations of odor structure and valence, especially in the amygdala.
